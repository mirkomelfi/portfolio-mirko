About Me

I am an organized, meticulous person, always eager to learn. I work well both independently and in teams. Currently, I'm in the fifth and final year of my Software Engineering degree, having completed over 85% of the subjects and earning the Intermediate Degree as an IT Analyst. In my free time, I seek to continue learning new technologies and deepening my knowledge on the ones I already know. I have C1 level of English, and I am still studying English with the goal of reaching C2 level.

Beyond my academic background, I lead two personal projects that showcase my entrepreneurial spirit. Since the age of 17, I have managed a dog training business, personally handling client communication, understanding their needs, and conducting classes, achieving over 1000 successful cases with proven results. This experience has honed my ability to explain concepts, build trust, and maintain effective communication with diverse audiences. Part of this journey can be seen on the business website.

More recently, after years of freelancing, I founded a web design studio where we develop modern websites for professionals and small businesses, applying best practices in UX/UI, responsive design, and SEO. In this role, I not only handle the technical aspects but also sell projects, negotiate budgets, communicate with clients, and ensure a clear, approachable, and professional experience, as well as leading the team working with me. This has enhanced my commercial profile, persuasion skills, and human approach, which I consider key in any professional setting.
Both experiences have strengthened my organizational, leadership, and communication skills, complementing my academic background in computer science.
